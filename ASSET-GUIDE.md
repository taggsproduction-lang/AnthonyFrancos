# Anthony Franco's — Asset Drop Guide

Drop your files into the folders below. These assets will power the new Next.js site.

---

## `assets/branding/logos/`
**What to drop:** All logo variations
- Primary logo (full color)
- White/light version (for dark backgrounds)
- Dark version (for light backgrounds)
- Icon-only mark (no text)
- **Preferred formats:** SVG (best), PNG with transparent background (min 1000px wide)
- **Bonus:** Any brand guidelines PDF you have

## `assets/branding/favicon/`
**What to drop:** Favicon source file
- Square logo/icon at 512x512px minimum (PNG or SVG)
- We'll generate all favicon sizes from this

## `assets/branding/fonts/`
**What to drop:** Any custom brand fonts
- .woff2, .woff, .ttf, or .otf files
- If using Google Fonts, just tell me the names — no files needed

## `assets/photography/food/`
**What to drop:** Hero-quality food shots — this is the #1 conversion driver
- Pizza beauty shots (whole pies, slices with cheese pull)
- Signature dishes / best sellers
- Appetizers, pasta, anything photogenic
- **Specs:** Landscape orientation preferred, minimum 1920px wide, high resolution
- **Priority items:** Your top 5 most-ordered dishes

## `assets/photography/interior/`
**What to drop:** Inside the restaurant
- Dining room, bar area, booths
- Warm lighting shots that show atmosphere
- Any shots showing it busy/lively (social proof)

## `assets/photography/exterior/`
**What to drop:** Storefront and outside
- Building front with signage visible
- Outdoor seating if applicable
- Street view / approach shot

## `assets/photography/team/`
**What to drop:** People shots
- Owner/chef portrait (builds trust — huge for local SEO)
- Kitchen action shots (tossing dough, pulling pies from oven)
- Team photos

## `assets/photography/ambiance/`
**What to drop:** Mood/vibe shots
- Close-ups: flour-dusted hands, bubbling cheese, wood-fired flames
- Anything that evokes the sensory experience of eating there

## `assets/video/hero/`
**What to drop:** Background video for the hero section
- 10-20 second loop, no audio needed
- Pizza being made, cheese pull, oven shots
- **Specs:** 1920x1080 minimum, MP4 format, keep under 15MB (we'll compress)

## `assets/video/promo/`
**What to drop:** Any promo/hype videos
- Restaurant overview, customer testimonials on video, events

## `assets/menu/`
**What to drop:** Current menu data
- PDF of current menu
- Or a spreadsheet/text file with: item names, descriptions, prices, categories
- Flag which items are best sellers or chef's picks
- Note any dietary labels (GF, vegan, etc.)

## `assets/testimonials/`
**What to drop:** Social proof
- Screenshots of Google/Yelp reviews (the best ones)
- Any customer quotes you have permission to use
- Awards, press mentions, "Best of" badges

## `assets/social/`
**What to drop:** Social media assets
- Links to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok (just drop a text file with URLs)
- Any existing social content you want featured on the site

## `assets/icons/`
**What to drop:** Custom icons if any
- If you have custom illustrated icons for delivery, dine-in, etc.
- Otherwise we'll use a professional icon set

---

## Priority Order (what to get first)

1. **Logos** — we can't design without them
2. **Food photography** — the single biggest conversion factor
3. **Menu data** — core content of the site
4. **Hero video** — major competitive differentiator
5. **Everything else** — layer in as available
